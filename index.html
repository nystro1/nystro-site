Before you begin

Tier 01 — a 12-hour dry fast with no dietary restrictions — is accessible to most healthy adults. The body already does this naturally overnight. You are simply extending and making it intentional. If you have any underlying health conditions, consult your doctor before beginning. When in doubt, start at Tier 01 and build from there.

Not at Tier 01. Whether you eat meat, fish, dairy or anything else — no dietary changes are required to begin. The fast is the medicine, not the diet. Tier 02 moves to plant-based eating in the window for those ready to go deeper.
